# 🧾 Institutional Pilot Onboarding Framework

The onboarding protocol for Sher AI Institutional Nodes.

## 🏁 Phase 1: Qualification (Week 1)
- **KYC/B Audit**: Verification of institutional identity and compliance framework.
- **Use-Case Mapping**: Defining alpha sharding vs. research-only scope.
- **NDA Protocol**: Standard institutional data-protection handshake.

## 🧪 Phase 2: Sandbox Provisioning (Week 2)
- **Identity Shard**: Creation of a restricted-role identity (MASTER_NODE_CLIENT_X).
- **Simulation Node**: Deployment of a dedicated Cloud Run sandbox for the client.
- **Veto Training**: Client walkthrough of the "No-Trade" decision logic.

## 📡 Phase 3: Shadow Execution (Week 3-4)
- **Live Feed Access**: 100% real-time market data ingestion.
- **Logic Validation**: Weekly audit of AI behaviour vs. client expectations.
- **Discipline Check**: Reviewing the "Survival Score" and "Veto Efficiency."

## 🏛️ Phase 4: Production Conversion
- **License Handshake**: Annual SaaS / On-prem licensing commit.
- **Shard Transition**: Promoting logic nodes from Shadow to Production.
- **SLA Commitment**: Formal activation of the 99.9% uptime guarantee.
